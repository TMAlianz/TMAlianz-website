Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What services do you provide?

We offer a wide range of services including:

  • Man and Van Services
  • Home Removals
  • Office Relocations
  • Waste Clearance
  • Single Item Moves
  • London to Manchester Bi-weekly Routes

2. How do I book a service?

Visit the Customer page, select the service you need, and submit your details. Our team will get in touch to confirm your booking.

3. Do you offer same-day services?

Yes, depending on availability. Contact us to check for urgent bookings.

4. How do I get a quote?

Submit your details through our booking form, and we’ll send you a personalized quote.

5. What areas do you cover?

We primarily serve London and the surrounding areas but can cover other UK locations upon request.

6. What should I do if I need to cancel?

Please notify us at least 24 hours before your scheduled service to avoid cancellation fees.
