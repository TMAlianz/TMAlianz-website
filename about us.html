About Us

TMAlianz is a family-run business based in London. We started as a small courier company, providing efficient and reliable delivery services across London and beyond. Over time, we evolved into a full-service "man and van" business, offering home and office removals, waste clearance, and much more.

At TMAlianz, we believe moving and delivery should be stress-free and personal. That's why we treat every job like it's our own — with care, communication, and attention to detail. Our mission is to simplify your journey, whether it's a new home, a fresh start, or just getting something from point A to B.

We proudly serve all of London and the surrounding areas, including airport collections and long-distance relocations. If you're unsure whether we cover your location — just ask!

Our commitment to outstanding customer service has earned us a loyal customer base. With hundreds of satisfied clients, we pride ourselves on professionalism, reliability, and competitive pricing.

Our Promise: No hidden fees, no confusing terms — just honest work, fair prices, and a team that cares.
